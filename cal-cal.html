Extra Info

Reminder! Be careful when dieting. If you are seeing a lose of more than two poounds over the cours of a week it could be a sign that you are over doing it and depriving your body of its necessary energy intake.

  • Gain weight is Calculated for about a pound every twelve days so as to prevent to much bloating.

  • Maintain weight is exactly what it sounds like being set at the amount of calories you need to stay at your current weight

  • Mild weight loss is set for a rate of about half a pound a week.

  • Weight loss is set for a rate of about a pound a week

  • Extreme weight loss is set for a rate of about two pounds a week (not advisable for most)
