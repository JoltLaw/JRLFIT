Pyramids are a great way to add volume and intensity into your workout. They use a changing weight and rep scheme to make sure that you're properly pushing yourself

Do to how useful Pyramids are I figured I should include a calculator so that you can plot out your own.

Extra Info

Reminder! Be careful when lifting. Remember to take long enough rest periods inbetween sets to reduce risk of injury as low as possible.

  • Light weightWhen lifting in a range of around ten reps with a light weight it is recommended you take about a two minute rest period between Sets

  • Moderate weight When lifting in the range of around eight reps with a moderate weight it is recommended to take a two to three minute rest period between sets

  • Heavy weightWhen lifting in the range of around five reps with a heavy weight it is recommended to take three to five minute rest periods between sets

  • Ultra Heavy weightWhen lifting in the range of around three reps with an ultra heavy weight it is recommended to take four to to five minute rest periods between sets
